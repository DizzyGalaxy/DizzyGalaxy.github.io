My Artworks

"I draw not just for my games, but for fun as well. Ever since I was young, I loved drawing. I always experiment with new art styles, I especially like drawing pixel art. Now, I'm actually drawing for my games to pursue my dream ever further."

Eevee in a Switch 2

"My brother just beat Pokemon: Lets Go Eevee recently and I immediately thought about drawing Eevee jumping out of the newly released Switch 2. I especially love how the grass pops out of the console."

Kirby (Mario Strikers Style)

"I have always loved the Mario Strikers Style. I think it looks so cool. So I tried to replicate it. I love how I can turn a cute creature to something so cool."

Me as a Ninja

"When I saw the Minecraft manga, the characters looked so cool. One of them was even a Ninja. So I drew myself as a Ninja."
